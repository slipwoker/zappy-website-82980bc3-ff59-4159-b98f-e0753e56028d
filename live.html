How a local story becomes a live update

Our process ensures every broadcast delivers verified, timely information that Portsmouth residents can trust. From initial sourcing to multi-platform distribution, we maintain the highest standards of journalism.

Verify and source locally

Every story begins with rigorous fact-checking and verification from trusted local sources. Our team confirms details with multiple contacts before any information reaches our teleprompter, ensuring Portsmouth gets accurate news first.

2

Prepare scripts and teleprompter copy

Our editorial team crafts clear, concise scripts that deliver context and clarity. Each segment is timed, reviewed, and loaded into our teleprompter system to ensure smooth, professional delivery during live news broadcasting.
